# DESIGN DIRECTIVES
Vibe: Institutional, culturally authoritative, historical, and deeply Maharashtrian. 
Restriction: ZERO rounded bubbly borders, ZERO generic tech startup aesthetics. Use sharp or very slightly rounded corners (max 4px). Even though the colors are bright (Orange/Gold), the layout MUST remain rigid, academic, and structured.

# COLOR PALETTE
Background: #FAF9F6 (Warm Alabaster / Parchment)
Primary Brand: #D95319 (Traditional Saffron / Deep Marigold Orange)
Accent: #C5A059 (Antique Matte Gold)
Text: #2A1610 (Deep Espresso / Off-Black for high contrast)
Dark Mode / Footer Panels: #4A2511 (Deep Earthy Brown)

# TYPOGRAPHY
Headings: 'Playfair Display' or 'Merriweather' (Heavy, elegant Serif).
Body/Buttons/UI: 'Inter' or 'Lato' (Clean Sans-Serif).

# UI ELEMENT RULES
Buttons: Sharp corners, solid Primary Orange or Gold with dark text. No gradient buttons.
Cards/Containers: Earthy brown or parchment backgrounds with thin gold borders (1px solid #C5A059).
Shadows: Keep shadows tight and dark; avoid large, diffused, soft colorful glows.
