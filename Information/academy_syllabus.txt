# SWARSANDHYA SANGEET NIKETAN
The educational wing of the Sangh, affiliated with the prestigious Akhil Bharatiya Gandharva Mahavidyalaya[cite: 1].
Purpose: To provide formal, classical musical education to folk artists[cite: 1].

# COURSE CATEGORIES
1. Classical & Light Music: Classical Singing, Semi-Vocal Singing, Bhajan, Thumri, Ghazal[cite: 1].
2. Voice Culture: Specialized 3-month certificate course[cite: 1].
3. Folk Arts: Folk Singing and Folk Percussion (Dholki, Sambal, Tashya, Shahiri Daf)[cite: 1].
4. Instrumental: Harmonium, Synthesizer, Piano, Flute, Guitar, Tabla, and Mrudang[cite: 1].
5. Dance: Classical Kathak and Modern Dance[cite: 1].

# LOGISTICS
Classes are held offline twice a week, with online options available on demand[cite: 1].
