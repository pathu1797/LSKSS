# INTERNATIONAL TOURS
In 2024, the Sangh took Maharashtrian folk music to the United States via the "Harirang Concert"[cite: 1, 2]. 
They performed the traditional "Devicha Gondhal" in cities including New Jersey, Philadelphia, Edison, Austin, New York, and Boston[cite: 1].
A major milestone was performing at the Philadelphia Ganesh Festival, North America's largest 10-day public Ganesh festival[cite: 2].

# PM MODI EVENT
The Shinde brothers performed the traditional Jagran Gondhal in the United States in the presence of Prime Minister Narendra Modi during his September tour[cite: 1, 5].

# CINEMATIC & AWARD CREDITS (Prof. Haridas Shinde)
Awards: Lokmanya Tilak Award, Annabhau Sathe Award, Swami Vivekananda Award (2012), Ananya Sanman (2017), and Sargam Sanman (2019)[cite: 1, 5].
Film Playback: Contributed to major Marathi films including Fatteshikast, Pawankhind, Hambirrao, Anandi Gopal, and Ek Hota Malin[cite: 5].
