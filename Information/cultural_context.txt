# INSTITUTIONAL BACKGROUND
Organization: Lok Sanskruti Kala Seva Sangh, established August 15, 2022, in Ahilyanagar (Ahmednagar)[cite: 1].
Founders: Prof. Pandurang Uttam Shinde and Prof. Haridas Shinde[cite: 1].
Mission: Conservation, development, and global promotion of national folk art and culture[cite: 1].

# THE 17TH GENERATION LEGACY
The founders are the 17th descendants of Gondhali Agyandas, the legendary Shahir who was in the court of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj[cite: 2, 3, 5]. 

# THE FATHER'S INFLUENCE
They inherited the art from their father, the late H.B.P. Purushottam Maharaj Shinde (Hatwalankar)[cite: 4]. He transformed the traditional Gondhali art form by uniting it with the spiritual Warkari path, living a life of deep devotion until his passing at age 73[cite: 4]. 

# SOCIAL IMPACT
The organization actively engages in social welfare, including organizing free mask distribution programs for senior citizens and the needy during the COVID-19 pandemic[cite: 1]. They also created the first "Randhumali Sambal Pathak" in 2017 to provide employment to folk artists[cite: 5].
